Our Story

Coffee worth waking up for

Chopper Coffee started with a single hand grinder, a bag of Ethiopian beans, and a stubborn belief that great coffee deserves great company. Today we source directly from farmers who share our obsession with quality — and we brew every cup like it's the first one.
